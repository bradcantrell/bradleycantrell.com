# Failure
**Year:** 2019–2020
**Type:** Drawing / Exhibition / Film
**Location:** Drawing Codes, Pratt (2019); Storm Signals, Chicago Architecture Biennial (2020)
**Collaborators:** Emma Mendel
**Phase:** Phase IV — Cultivated Wildness (2017–2025)

## Description

Code-generated urban form as base layer. Subsequent layers added through irregular schedules and varied media, with each collaborator building upon, erasing, or contorting the other's work. No layer sacred. Storm Signals film catalogued environmental failures across two centuries, presenting failure not as aberration but as a constituent element of environmental history.

## Contribution

Failure as landscape condition: failure is, at the heart of landscape, environments inherently complex, multi-valent relationships in constant flux. Anti-fragility: how to develop systems that more readily recover from disturbance, shifting the design focus from preventing failure to enabling recovery. Process over product: meaning emerges through accumulation and relation.
