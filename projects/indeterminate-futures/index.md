# Indeterminate Futures
**Year:** 2021
**Type:** Digital Exhibition / NFT Archive
**Location:** 17th Venice Architecture Biennale (digital component)
**Collaborators:** Xun Liu
**Phase:** Phase IV — Cultivated Wildness (2017–2025)

## Description

Years of geomorphology table documentation, thousands of 15–30 second video increments of sediment sorting, channel migration, and delta formation, minted incrementally as NFTs on Tezos blockchain throughout the Biennale. Each token: a unique morphological configuration that would never reoccur, even if the same parameters were repeated. The archive grew throughout the Biennale, and the catalog itself exhibited the temporal dynamics being researched. Visitors could collect tokens, becoming stakeholders in the archive.

## Contribution

Representing the unrepresentable: instead of seeking a definitive image, the project produced an expanding archive of singular moments. Representation becomes accumulative rather than definitive, serial rather than singular. Notation as interpretation: documentation is never neutral recording but always interpretive construction. Each experiment followed protocols, yet each produced unique configurations.
