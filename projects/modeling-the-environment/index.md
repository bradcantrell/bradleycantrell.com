# Modeling the Environment
**Year:** 2012
**Type:** Publication
**Collaborators:** Natalie Yates
**Publisher:** Wiley
**Phase:** Phase I — The Digital Threshold (2005–2012)

## Description

Introduced the critical dimension of time to landscape representation. Procedural modeling of terrain that could erode, simulation of forest growth across decades with ecologically-driven planting plans, and GIS data integrated directly into design models, transforming the digital model from a picture of the site into a database of the site. Representation of seasonal change, growth patterns, and material weathering across multiple temporal scales.

## Contribution

Landscape representation must engage temporal depth: a building is finished when construction ends, a landscape is just beginning. GIS integration shifts the model from picture to instrument, and design simulation becomes exploration rather than visualization.
